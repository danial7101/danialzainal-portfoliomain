UKM OUTLET

An online shopping platform for Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia students

developed from scratch by a group of 5

Project Description

Project Description

UKM Outlet is an online shopping centre for Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia students. Currently, most UKM students only sell goods or products that they produce manually, i.e. filling in a Google Form to order products, making the method traditional and less efficient. To ensure the success of this project, several studies have been done to achieve it. We used a descriptive research method where we did some research and studies as an instrument for data collection by using Google Form. This system implements the use of various website development languages as the main material to develop this system. The results of this study are the basis for creating a simpler system and improving the user experience. This system allows users and sellers who are both UKM students to collaborate by selling and buying each other's products. Finally, with the existence of UKM Outlet, UKM students no longer need to sell and buy goods in the traditional way and will make a great contribution to the entrepreneurs who are among the students in UKM.
