Attack on Ultron

A short 3D animated video for the 3D Modeling course final project

Synopsis

Synopsis

Ultron, the rogue AI, has unleashed chaos, attacking civilians with his relentless robotic army. As cities fall and the skies darken, the police and military struggle to contain the onslaught. Amidst the devastation, a small group of unlikely heroes emerges, uncovering a secret that could change everything. In a desperate bid to save humanity, they confront their fears and form unexpected alliances. The battle of man versus machine has begun, and the fate of the world hangs in the balance.
